Doteveryone Trustee

Doteveryone exists to understand and address the new set of moral and social challenges that has arrived with the internet, to help make life fairer and simpler for everyone in the UK. We do that by making sure everyone has the digital skills to thrive in the network age, and by building examples of great services that can influence and shape our future, ensuring that technology works for everyone.

Person Specification

We are looking for three trustees with expertise in one or more of the following areas:

Civic tech / social innovation

We’re pretty digital people and we’re connected in areas like technology, business, government and services. But we’d love to have better connections in areas like ‘civic tech’ and ‘social innovation’. We want to make sure we’re partnering with and helping the best practitioners in these areas - can you help us connect? Please consider becoming a trustee

Law

We’d love to find someone knowledgeable in the practise of law, and in particular in charitable law. Someone who can you advise on good governance, and help us to better manage the risks that are associated with running a small and ambitious charity. Particularly, we’d like to find someone who can help us work out how charities should flex and adapt their structures so they can be as agile as a start-up but stay true to their charitable purposes.

Strategy & Influencing

We’re ambitious about our impact. We want to influence mainstream opinion. That means we’re keen to get guidance and advice about what to say and how best to say it. Can you help us get better at communicating? At honing and focusing our messages?

Benefits

  • Trustees will be recruited for a 12 month term, which may then be renewed.
  • The positions are unpaid, but reasonable expenses will be paid.
  • Doteveryone is equal opportunities employer; we believe that diverse teams create better work and as such we encourage a broad range of perspectives, approaches and backgrounds in applicants.

Suitable applicants will be shortlisted and invited to interview with the CEO Rachel Coldicutt during the week beginning November 14th, 2016. Second interviews will take place with the Doteveryone Chair, Martha Lane Fox, on November 22nd, 2016. Please submit your application with a CV and a cover letter explaining your suitability in relation to the areas outlined above. Applications close November 6th, 2016.
