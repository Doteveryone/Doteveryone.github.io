Networks & Engagement Lead

Doteveryone is hiring a Networks & Engagement Lead.

This is a part time role (3 days per week), for an experienced networks person to lead on building networks, engagement within the civic tech / social innovation sector and curation of events. The role is available for a 12 month fixed term.

You'll be responsible for:

  • Leading on relationship-building, specifically around the projects that we’re working on
  • Introducing creative ways to engage with our partners and wider networks
  • Designing feedback loops around each of the projects to ensure that we’re regularly sharing our work with our partners and wider networks
  • Designing and hosting events with our partners and wider networks,, at all levels and scale. For example, high profile showcase events and small, intimate breakfast events.
  • Attending events and other activities, horizon scanning and intelligence gathering, to maintain an awareness of relevant work and partners in the areas in which we work or are interested
  • Documenting, visualising and being generative around the connections you make and the value you are creating
  • Building a community of people we can draw on for different types of expertise and input for the work we are doing
  • Helping us understand how we can be useful to the wider community
  • Sharing discoveries and learning with the Doteveryone team
  • Designing and maintaining processes to find out from the team who it would be useful for them to connect to, learn from or spend time with.

Requirements

  • An excellent understanding of the themes that Doteveryone want to impact
  • An empathetic individual, great at building relationships, being authentic in your communications and getting on with people.
  • Able to communicate effectively with a diverse and eclectic range of people. This might be an MP, a nurse in a hospice, a corporate CEO or technology entrepreneur.
  • Highly organised, and able to follow up with people and organise events effectively and smoothly.
  • Excellent written, oral, presentation and interpersonal skills
  • Able to see opportunities for new work, relationships and collaborations.
  • Great at designing experiences that mean people get the most out of them (whether that be learning, new knowledge, feedback etc.)
  • Experience of designing and curating events that ensure diversity
  • Effective at sharing with a team, with a collaborative approach
  • A strong commitment to the values and goals of Doteveryone

BENEFITS

  • Competitive rate of pay
  • NEST Pension scheme
  • Part-time position
  • Flexible hours and some remote-working are possible for this role
